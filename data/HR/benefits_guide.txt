ACME Benefits Guide FY2026 (HR)

Health:
- Medical: Aetna PPO and HMO; ACME pays 90% of employee premium, 75% dependent.
- Dental: MetLife. Vision: VSP.
- Mental health: Spring Health, 12 free sessions per year.

Time off:
- PTO: 20 days/year, accrual-based, max carryover 10 days.
- Sick: 10 days/year, no accrual cap.
- Parental leave: 16 weeks paid for primary caregiver, 8 for secondary.
- Sabbatical: 4 weeks paid after 5 years of service.

Retirement:
- 401(k) with 4% match, vests immediately.
- ESPP: 15% discount, 6-month look-back.

This document is restricted to HR for confidential discussions about coverage tiers and edge cases.
