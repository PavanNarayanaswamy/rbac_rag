ACME Corporation - Company Overview (PUBLIC)

ACME Corporation is a global technology company founded in 2010, headquartered in San Francisco, California.
We build enterprise software for the manufacturing, logistics, and retail sectors.

Our mission: "Empower every business to operate intelligently."

Office locations: San Francisco (HQ), New York, London, Bangalore, Singapore.
Total employees worldwide: approximately 2,400.

Public values:
- Customer obsession
- Operational excellence
- Long-term thinking
- Trust and transparency
