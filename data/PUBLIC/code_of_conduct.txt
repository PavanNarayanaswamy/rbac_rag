TEST Code of Conduct (PUBLIC)

All employees, contractors, and interns must adhere to the following:

1. Treat colleagues, customers, and vendors with respect.
2. Protect confidential information and customer data.
3. Avoid conflicts of interest; disclose any potential conflicts to your manager.
4. No harassment, discrimination, or retaliation of any kind.
5. Comply with all applicable laws including export controls and anti-bribery rules.
6. Report concerns via the ethics hotline at ethics@TEST.example.com.

Violations may result in disciplinary action up to and including termination.
This document applies to everyone at TEST.
