Engineering On-Call Runbook (ENGG)

P0 incidents: respond within 5 minutes, ack via PagerDuty.
P1 incidents: respond within 15 minutes during business hours, 30 minutes off-hours.

Standard incident commands:
- kubectl rollout restart deployment/<name> -n prod
- kubectl logs -f deployment/<name> -n prod | tee /tmp/incident.log
- terraform plan -var-file=prod.tfvars (NEVER apply during an incident without IC approval)

Escalation tree:
1. Primary on-call (rotation in PagerDuty schedule "platform-primary")
2. Secondary on-call
3. Engineering Manager
4. VP Engineering

Postmortem template lives at docs/templates/postmortem.md. Blameless culture - root cause focus.
This runbook is restricted to Engineering staff only.
