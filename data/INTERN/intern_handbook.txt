ACME Intern Handbook (INTERN)

Welcome! Your internship runs 12 weeks with the option to extend.

Schedule:
- Week 1: Orientation, laptop setup, buddy assignment.
- Week 2-10: Project work with your team.
- Week 11: Final presentation rehearsal.
- Week 12: Final presentation to leadership and intern wrap party.

Resources available to you:
- Slack #interns-2026 channel
- Weekly intern lunch every Wednesday
- Monthly speaker series (past speakers: CTO, VP Product)
- LinkedIn Learning license (free)

Performance expectations:
- Weekly 1:1 with your manager
- Mid-internship review at week 6
- Final review and return-offer decision by week 10

This document is for interns only.
