Sales Pricing Playbook - FY26 (SALES)

List Pricing (USD/month):
- Starter: $499 (up to 25 seats)
- Growth: $1,999 (up to 100 seats)
- Enterprise: starts at $5,000, custom quote.

Discount Authority:
- AE: up to 10% off list.
- Sales Manager: up to 20% off list.
- VP Sales: up to 35% off list.
- Anything beyond requires CRO + CFO sign-off.

Standard contract term is 12 months, NET-30 payment.
Multi-year prepay: 8% additional discount for 24-month, 15% for 36-month.

Q4 SPIFF: 1.5x commission on Enterprise deals closed before Dec 15.
This document is restricted to the Sales organization.
